About

Alexander Ignatov a.k.a. sad3d is an independent game developer creating quiet, melancholic games. Rather than chasing spectacle, he creates slow-paced interactive experiences that invite players to observe, wander, and discover the extraordinary within the familiar. He has released three commercial games on Steam, including It's Winter, Routine Feat, and Neyasnoe.

Alongside making games, he has spent a decade training and working as a physician —
a parallel path that leaves its mark on everything he makes.

Currently based in Yerevan, Armenia.
